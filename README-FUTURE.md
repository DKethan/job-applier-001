## üîÆ Future Capabilities

### Phase 1: Making It Automatic (What We're Building Now!)

#### Super Smart Chrome Extension
- **Detects forms automatically** - Knows exactly which fields to fill on job sites
- **Handles complex applications** - Works with multi-page forms and tricky layouts
- **Maps your info perfectly** - Puts your details in the right places every time
- **Shows progress** - You can see exactly what's being filled out

#### One-Click Job Applications
- **Click and apply** - Submit complete applications with a single button
- **Apply to many jobs** - Handle multiple similar positions at once
- **Smart timing** - Send applications when hiring managers are most likely to see them
- **Auto follow-ups** - Send thank-you emails automatically

### Phase 2: Making It Super Smart (Next Level AI!)

#### AI That Gets Better and Better
- **Learns from success** - Gets smarter by seeing which applications work best
- **Knows the company** - Researches companies automatically for perfect cover letters
- **Salary intelligence** - Helps you understand market rates and negotiate better
- **Career guidance** - Shows you the best paths for your professional growth

#### Build Your Personal Brand
- **LinkedIn superpowers** - Sync with your profile and suggest connections
- **Professional website** - Auto-create beautiful portfolio sites
- **Content ideas** - Generate blog posts and social media content
- **Networking help** - Smart suggestions for who to connect with and how

### Phase 3: Big Team Power (For Companies & Recruiters)

#### Work Together as a Team
- **Team accounts** - Share profiles and collaborate on applications
- **Success analytics** - Track what's working and what's not
- **Recruiter features** - Tools to help hiring teams work better
- **ATS mastery** - Beat applicant tracking systems like a pro

#### Integration Ecosystem
- **Job Board APIs**: Direct integration with major job platforms
- **CRM Integration**: Connect with customer relationship management tools
- **Calendar Integration**: Interview scheduling and management
- **Document Management**: Integration with Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.
